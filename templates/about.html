Description:
This dashboard uses interactive visualization tools to demonstrate the different types of cancers and their prevalences across each province of Canada, from the 2016-2017 Canadian Cancer Society dataset. The map visualizes all of Canada with a dropdown menu of all cancers. Our dashboard also contains interactive bar charts including the top 10 most prevalent cancers in each province, including a comparison chart between male and female data.
